Manual Outreach Instructions

Back to Dashboard

Current Mode: MANUAL

All outreach templates are generated and ready to copy. This page provides step-by-step instructions for manually sending emails and SMS to your leads.

1 Email Outreach

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Go to the Outreach page in the CRM Control Panel (http://localhost:3000/outreach)
  2. Click on any lead to see their personalized email template
  3. Click the Copy button next to the email subject and body
  4. Open your email provider (Gmail, Outlook, etc.)
  5. Create a new email and paste the subject and body
  6. Replace [YOUR_DEMO_LINK] with the actual demo site URL
  7. Send the email to the lead
  8. Return to the CRM and mark the lead as "Contacted"

Tips:

  • - Send emails in batches of 10-15 per day to avoid spam filters
  • - Personalize the opening line with the lead's first name
  • - Always include the demo link - it's the visual proof of your work
  • - Follow up after 3 days if no response

2 SMS Outreach

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Find leads with phone numbers in the Outreach page
  2. Click on a lead to see their SMS template
  3. Copy the SMS message (already formatted under 160 characters)
  4. Open your SMS provider (Google Voice, Twilio, etc.)
  5. Send the SMS to the lead's phone number
  6. Replace [YOUR_DEMO_LINK] with the actual demo URL

SMS Character Limit:

SMS messages are capped at 160 characters. Messages over this limit will be split or truncated. The templates are pre-formatted to fit within this limit.

3 Bulk Export (CSV)

Export all leads with their email templates to a CSV file for bulk importing into your email platform.

  1. Click "Export CSV" button in the Outreach page
  2. Open the CSV in Excel or Google Sheets
  3. Review and edit any personalization fields
  4. Import into Mailchimp, SendGrid, or your email platform
  5. Send using the platform's bulk send feature

! Enabling Auto Mode

Auto mode is currently disabled. To enable automatic sending:

  1. Set up API keys in the server/.env file:

# Email (SendGrid)

SENDGRID_API_KEY=your_sendgrid_key


# SMS (Twilio)

TWILIO_ACCOUNT_SID=your_twilio_sid

TWILIO_AUTH_TOKEN=your_twilio_token

TWILIO_PHONE_NUMBER=+1234567890

  1. Update config/settings.py - Set AUTO_ENABLED = True
  2. Restart the server
  3. Toggle Auto Mode in the Outreach page

Important Compliance Notes

  • - Always include an unsubscribe option in emails
  • - Respect CAN-SPAM and GDPR regulations
  • - Only contact leads during reasonable hours (8am - 8pm local time)
  • - Keep records of all communications
  • - Honor unsubscribe requests immediately
